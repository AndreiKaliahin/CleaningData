"Subject"
	1 .. 30 - volunteers within an age bracket of 19-48 years

"Activity"
	Activities which were performed by volunteers

	* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	* SITTING

	* STANDING
	* LAYING 

"tBodyAcc_mean_X_Avg". Real.
	Average of means of time domain signals by X - Axis for every subject every activity which come from the accelerometer for "Body"* part of signal.
	* Using low pass Butterworth filter with a corner frequency of 0.3 Hz signals from accelerometer were separated to Body and Gravity parts
